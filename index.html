bikes for you

The Idea

  • Improve public transportation
  • Help tourism, economy and environment

How?

  • Free rental bikes / public transport
  • Vouchers for food, events, city attractions
  • Combined into a complete package / app

The APP

  • Connected to a PayPal account or credit card
  • Unlocks the free bikes
  • May include free bus rides, etc.
  • City attractions
  • Restaurants
  • Events: festival dates, concerts

Financing / Revenue

  • Public financing (tourism argument)
  • In-App Ads from local businesses
  • Listing-fee for companies
  • Indirectly by providing discounts
  • Premium members

Incentive for Companies / Cities

  • Mobile population
  • Less street weardown / maintenance
  • More customers for businesses
  • More life on the streets
  • More attractive to tourists
  • Connect the city / town businesses
